Platinum Hair and Beauty

Platinum Hair and Beauty Salon is a world-renowned salon specializing in a vast range of hair and beauty treatments. Jill Addison and her professional team of talented hairstylists and beauticians only use the highest quality products available on the market today.

For in-house treatments and retail, only salon professional products such as Schwarzkopf, Kerastase, Guinot, Dermalogica, and Environ are used at Platinum Hair and Beauty.

The salon has been successful for more than 23 years under Jill Addison's ownership, and is currently sponsoring a top national television program. With qualified staff members providing services for matriculants, weddings, makeovers, and TV actors, Platinum Hair and Beauty is dedicated to treating every client as royalty.
